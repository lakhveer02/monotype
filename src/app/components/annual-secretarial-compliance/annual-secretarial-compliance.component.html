Annual Secretarial Compliance

This page provides all the necessary details regarding the annual secretarial compliance for the company, including the compliance calendar, important deadlines, historical compliance data, and guidelines.

Compliance Overview

The annual secretarial compliance covers all mandatory filings and adherence to statutory regulations as required by the Companies Act. It includes ensuring that all necessary documents and returns are filed within the stipulated timelines.

Compliance Calendar

Month Compliance Due Description
January Annual Return Filing Submit the annual return for the previous financial year.
April Tax Filing File corporate tax returns for the last financial year.
July Shareholders Meeting Conduct the Annual General Meeting (AGM).
October Financial Audit Complete and submit the financial audit report.

Important Deadlines

  • March 31: Last date for filing the income tax return for the previous year.
  • September 30: Deadline for submitting the audited financial statements.
  • December 31: End of the financial year for annual reporting purposes.
