Scrutinizer Observations

The scrutinizer noted that all voting procedures were conducted in accordance with the regulations. However, it was observed that the participation rate was lower than expected, which could be improved in future meetings.

Recommendations for Future Meetings

  • Encourage greater shareholder participation by sending reminders well in advance.
  • Provide clear guidelines and explanations on the resolutions being voted upon.
  • Consider adopting electronic voting to facilitate easier participation
